Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams of spaghetti
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water for 10-12 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the pan and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Mix the cooked spaghetti with the sauce until well combined.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste, and serve.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (apple cider or white vinegar)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a round cake pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix the water, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and vinegar.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Allow the cake to cool before serving or frosting.

Vegan Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chickpeas (canned or cooked)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, tomato, red onion, olives, and chickpeas.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve fresh and enjoy!
